## 6. How to Use the Review Response Generator

The review response generator helps draft professional replies to customer reviews.

To use it:

1. Log in to the admin area.
2. Go to [Website URL]/review-generator.
3. Paste the customer review.
4. Select the star rating.
5. Choose a tone.
6. Confirm the business type and business name.
7. Click **Generate with AI**.
8. Review and edit the draft.
9. Copy the final version.
10. Manually post it on the review platform.

Important: the tool does not automatically post to Google, Yelp, Instagram, Facebook, or any other platform.

## 7. How to Use the Lead Follow-Up Generator

The lead follow-up generator helps draft responses to new inquiries.

To use it:

1. Log in to the admin area.
2. Go to [Website URL]/follow-up-generator.
3. Enter the lead name, goal, preferred service, timeline, and message.
4. Choose a tone.
5. Choose the channel: email, SMS, or Instagram DM.
6. Click **Generate follow-up**.
7. Review and edit the draft.
8. Copy the final version.
9. Manually send it through your normal email, phone, or social inbox.

Important: the tool does not automatically email, text, DM, or message customers.

Recommended monthly checks:

- Confirm the contact form still submits correctly.
- Review all new leads in the dashboard.
- Confirm owner email notifications are arriving.

## 12. Troubleshooting Basics

If a lead form is not working:

- Confirm the required fields are filled in.
- Try a different browser or device.
- Check whether Supabase/database settings are active.
- Contact support with the time of the test and the email address used.

If owner emails are not arriving:

- Check spam or promotions folders.
- Confirm the owner notification email address is correct.
- Confirm the sending domain or email provider is active.
- Contact support if leads save but emails do not arrive.

If admin login is not working:

- Confirm you are using the correct admin URL.
- Confirm the admin password was entered exactly.
- Try logging out and logging in again.
- Contact support if the password needs to be reset.

If AI tools are not generating:

- Confirm you are logged in.
- Add enough context in the form fields.
- Try again after a few minutes if the provider is rate-limited.
- Contact support if the tool shows a configuration error.

## 13. AI Text Review Reminder

AI-generated text is a draft only. Always review and edit it before sending or posting.

Before using an AI draft, check that it:

- Sounds like your business
- Is accurate
- Does not promise specific results
- Does not include medical or legal advice
- Does not include private customer information
- Matches your actual services, pricing, schedule, and policies

## 14. AI Tools Do Not Automatically Post or Message

The AI tools in this website only create drafts.

They do not:

- Post review responses
- Send emails
- Send SMS messages
- Send Instagram DMs
- Contact customers automatically
- Publish content to third-party platforms

Your team remains responsible for reviewing, approving, and manually sending or posting any message.
