## Overview

[Client Business Name] wants a more polished, mobile-friendly way to capture local interest, guide visitors toward a consultation or inquiry, and respond faster to new leads and reviews.

ClientFlow AI Setup will provide a streamlined lead-booking website experience and simple AI-assisted tools designed specifically for local fitness and wellness businesses.

## Recommended Package

ClientFlow AI Setup - Starter Package

## Included Deliverables

The starter package includes:

- Mobile-first landing page or homepage refresh
- Lead capture form
- Lead tracker/dashboard
- Owner email notifications for new lead submissions
- AI review response generator
- AI lead follow-up generator
- Basic SEO setup
- One revision round
- Client handoff guide

## Project Goals

The goal of this setup is to help [Client Business Name]:

- Present a more professional first impression online
- Make the next step clear for mobile visitors
- Capture inquiries through a simple lead form
- Track new leads in one place
- Receive owner notifications when new leads submit
- Draft review responses faster
- Draft follow-up messages faster
- Launch with basic local SEO foundations in place

## What Is Not Included

This starter package does not include:

- Guaranteed leads or sales
- Full SEO campaign
- Paid ads
- Social media management
- Automatic posting to review platforms
- Automatic Instagram DMs
- SMS campaigns unless separately scoped
- Full custom CRM
- Mobile app
- Unlimited revisions

Any additional items can be quoted separately.

## Revision Policy

One revision round is included.

The included revision round may cover:

- Copy edits
- Small layout changes
- CTA wording changes
- Service wording changes
- Image swaps using client-provided assets
- Contact detail corrections

The revision round does not include:

- Full redesign
- New brand identity
- New major features
- New integrations
- Unlimited changes

Additional revision rounds can be quoted separately.

## AI Tool Disclaimer

The AI review response generator and AI lead follow-up generator create draft text only. The tools do not automatically post reviews, send Instagram messages, send SMS campaigns, or message customers.

The business owner is responsible for reviewing and editing all AI-generated text before using it.

AI-generated drafts should not be treated as legal, medical, clinical, or compliance advice.
