# Support Boundaries

Use this document to set clear support expectations with clients.

## Support Purpose

Support is provided to help with the ClientFlow AI setup that was delivered as part of the agreed project scope.

Support is not unlimited technical support for every tool, device, platform, or marketing channel the business uses.

## Included Support During Project

During the active project, support includes:

- Questions about the project scope
- Questions about required client content
- Clarifying feedback for the included revision round
- Launch coordination
- Basic explanation of the lead tracker
- Basic explanation of the AI draft tools

## Included Support After Launch

After launch, support depends on whether the client has an active maintenance plan.

Without a maintenance plan, support may include:

- Clarifying handoff instructions
- Answering reasonable launch-related questions for a short post-launch period
- Fixing confirmed launch issues caused by the original setup

With a maintenance plan, support follows the maintenance plan scope.

## Not Included In Support

Support does not include:

- Unlimited revisions
- Full redesigns
- New features
- Paid ads management
- Social media management
- Full SEO campaigns
- Third-party platform troubleshooting unrelated to the setup
- Device-specific troubleshooting
- Training every staff member individually
- Emergency support unless separately agreed
- Legal, medical, tax, or compliance advice

## AI Tool Boundaries

The AI tools create draft text only.

They do not:

- Automatically post review responses
- Automatically send Instagram DMs
- Automatically send SMS messages
- Replace owner judgment
- Provide legal advice
- Provide medical advice
- Guarantee results

The business owner is responsible for reviewing and approving all AI-generated text before using it.

A request may need a new quote if it requires:

- New design work
- New integrations
- New automations
- New pages
- Significant copywriting
- Custom CRM features
- Ongoing marketing strategy
- Work outside the original scope

## Plain-English Summary

Support covers the setup we agreed to build. New features, ongoing marketing, platform management, and unlimited changes are not included unless they are separately scoped.
