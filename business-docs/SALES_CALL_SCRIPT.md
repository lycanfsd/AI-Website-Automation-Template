# Sales Call Script

Use this script for discovery calls with fitness and wellness businesses.

## Call Goal

The goal of the call is to understand the business, confirm whether ClientFlow AI Setup is a good fit, explain the starter package clearly, and identify the next step.

## Opening

"Thanks for taking the time to chat. I want to learn a little about your business, what you are trying to improve online, and whether this setup would actually help. If it feels like a fit, I can walk you through the starter package and next steps."

## Quick Context

"ClientFlow AI Setup is built for local fitness and wellness businesses that want a cleaner mobile-first website experience, a lead capture form, a simple lead tracker, owner notifications, and AI-assisted drafting tools for review responses and lead follow-up."

"It is not a full marketing agency package. It does not include paid ads, guaranteed leads, or full SEO campaigns. It is meant to give you a stronger foundation for capturing and responding to local interest."

## Discovery Questions

### Business

"Can you tell me what type of business you run and who you mainly serve?"

"What are your main services right now?"

"Which service or offer do you most want new clients to inquire about?"

### Current Website

"Do you have a website right now?"

"What do you like or dislike about it?"

"Are most people finding you from Google, Instagram, referrals, or somewhere else?"

### Lead Flow

"When someone is interested, what do they usually do now?"

"Do they call, DM, email, fill out a form, or book directly?"

"Where do those leads go after they reach out?"

"How quickly are you usually able to respond?"

### Pain Points

"What feels most frustrating about your current online setup?"

"Are leads slipping through the cracks?"

"Do you feel like your website reflects the quality of your business?"

"Do you have a consistent way to respond to reviews and inquiries?"

### Decision Criteria

"If we improved this, what would success look like for you?"

"Do you need this launched by a specific date?"

"Who else needs to approve the project?"

## Positioning The Offer

"Based on what you shared, this sounds like the kind of setup that could help you look more polished online and make the inquiry process easier."

"The starter package includes a mobile-first landing page or homepage refresh, lead capture form, lead tracker, owner email notifications, AI review response generator, AI lead follow-up generator, basic SEO setup, one revision round, and a client handoff guide."

"The goal is not to promise a certain number of leads. The goal is to make sure that when someone does check you out, the next step is clear and your team has a simple way to respond."

## Clear Boundaries

"Just so expectations are clear, this package does not include paid ads, a full SEO campaign, social media management, automatic review posting, automatic Instagram DMs, SMS campaigns, a full custom CRM, or unlimited revisions. Any of those can be discussed separately if needed."

## Common Objections

### "Will this get me more leads?"

"It can help improve the path from visitor to inquiry, but I do not guarantee a specific number of leads or sales. Traffic, offer, reputation, follow-up speed, market demand, and pricing all affect results."

### "Can AI respond automatically?"

"For this starter package, no. The AI tools draft responses for you to review, edit, and use manually. They do not automatically post to Google, Yelp, Instagram, or message customers."

### "Can you do SEO?"

"This includes basic on-page SEO setup: page titles, descriptions, local business schema, sitemap, robots file, and crawlable contact info. It is not a full ongoing SEO campaign."

### "Can you connect SMS?"

"SMS campaigns are not included in the starter package. They can be scoped separately because they require compliance, opt-in language, provider setup, and additional testing."

## Close

"If this feels like the right fit, the next step is for me to send over the proposal and intake form. Once I have the intake details, assets, and approval to begin, I can start setting up the beta version."

## Next Step Options

- Send proposal
- Send intake form
- Schedule a follow-up call
- Recommend a different service if this is not a fit

## Follow-Up Message

"Thanks again for the call. Based on what we discussed, I think ClientFlow AI Setup could be a strong fit for helping your business look more polished online and capture inquiries more clearly. I am sending the proposal and intake form now. Once those are complete, I can confirm the project timeline and next steps."
