# Service Scope: ClientFlow AI Setup

Use this document to explain exactly what is included in the starter package for a fitness or wellness business.

## Service Overview

ClientFlow AI Setup is a done-for-you website and lead-response setup for local fitness and wellness businesses. It is designed to help the business look more professional online, capture new inquiries, follow up faster, and manage review responses with AI-assisted drafting tools.

This service is best for:

- Personal trainers
- Gyms and boutique fitness studios
- Yoga and Pilates studios
- Nutrition and wellness coaches
- Med spas
- Chiropractors
- Physical therapy clinics
- Other local wellness providers

## Starter Package Includes

- Mobile-first landing page or homepage refresh
- Lead capture form
- Lead tracker/dashboard
- Owner email notifications for new inquiries
- AI review response generator
- AI lead follow-up generator
- Basic on-page SEO setup
- One revision round
- Client handoff guide

## What The Client Receives

The client receives a polished lead-focused website experience that can be customized to their brand, services, location, and calls to action.

The setup is built to help visitors:

- Understand what the business offers
- Feel confident that the business is professional and trustworthy
- Submit an inquiry or book a consultation
- Receive timely follow-up from the business owner or team

The business owner receives simple admin tools to:

- View submitted leads
- Track lead status
- Add internal notes
- Draft review responses
- Draft lead follow-up messages

## What Is Not Included

The starter package does not include:

- Guaranteed leads or sales
- Full SEO campaign
- Paid ads
- Social media management
- Automatic posting to Google, Yelp, Instagram, or other review platforms
- Automatic Instagram DMs
- SMS campaigns unless separately scoped
- Full custom CRM
- Mobile app
- Unlimited revisions
- Ongoing support unless a maintenance plan is purchased

## Revision Round

One revision round is included after the first completed version is delivered.

A revision round may include reasonable updates such as:

- Text edits
- Service wording changes
- CTA wording changes
- Small layout adjustments
- Contact detail updates
- Image swaps if client-provided images are available

A revision round does not include:

- A full redesign
- A new brand identity
- Rebuilding the site for a different business model
- Adding new major features
- Adding third-party automations not previously scoped

## Client Responsibilities

The client is responsible for providing:

- Accurate business name, address, phone, email, and service area
- Service descriptions
- Approved testimonials or reviews
- Logo and brand assets, if available
- Photos, if available
- Legal, medical, or compliance disclaimers specific to their business
- Final review and approval before launch

The client is also responsible for reviewing AI-generated drafts before posting or sending them.

## Important Disclaimers

ClientFlow AI Setup does not guarantee leads, sales, rankings, weight loss results, health outcomes, or customer conversions.

AI-generated review responses and lead follow-ups are drafts only. The business owner must review, edit, and approve all AI-generated text before using it.

The website and AI tools do not provide medical advice, legal advice, diagnosis, treatment instructions, or guaranteed outcomes.

Optional add-ons can be scoped separately:

- Additional revision rounds
- Extra pages

- Paid ads landing page
