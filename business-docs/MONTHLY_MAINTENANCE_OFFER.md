## Included Monthly Support

The maintenance plan may include:

- Website uptime and form check
- Lead form test submission
- Owner notification check
- Small copy updates
- Contact detail updates
- Service or CTA wording updates
- Basic plugin/dependency review if applicable
- Privacy, terms, or disclaimer reminder review
- Monthly summary email
- One small support request per month

## What Counts As A Small Update

Examples:

- Updating phone number or email
- Changing business hours
- Swapping one testimonial
- Updating one service description
- Changing CTA wording
- Replacing one image
- Fixing a small typo

## Not Included In Maintenance

Monthly maintenance does not include:

- Full redesign
- New major pages
- Paid ads
- Full SEO campaign
- Social media management
- SMS campaigns
- New CRM build
- Custom app development
- Emergency support outside agreed hours
- Unlimited requests

Larger updates can be quoted separately.

## Optional Monthly Reporting

If analytics are configured, a simple monthly report can include:

- Website traffic snapshot
- Top pages
- Lead form submissions
- Basic conversion notes
- Recommended next steps

Reporting requires analytics setup and may be quoted separately.

## Why Maintenance Matters

After launch, small details can change quickly:

- Business hours
- Services
- Staff
- Offers
- Booking links
- Legal disclaimers
- Email routing

Maintenance helps keep the website accurate and reduces the chance that leads are missed due to outdated information.

## Suggested Client Pitch

"Your ClientFlow AI setup is live, but it still needs occasional care. The monthly maintenance plan keeps the lead form, notifications, content, and basic setup checked so you can focus on serving clients instead of managing website details."
