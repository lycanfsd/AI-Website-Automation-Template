# Revision Policy

Use this policy to set clear expectations before the first version is delivered.

## Included Revision Round

The ClientFlow AI Setup starter package includes one revision round.

A revision round is one organized list of requested changes submitted after the first version is delivered.

## What Counts As A Revision

Examples of included revisions:

- Updating text
- Adjusting CTA wording
- Changing service descriptions
- Swapping client-provided images
- Correcting contact information
- Adjusting section order
- Making small layout refinements
- Updating testimonials or FAQs

## What Does Not Count As A Small Revision

The included revision round does not include:

- Full redesign
- New brand identity
- Major new page layouts
- New custom features
- Third-party integrations not previously scoped
- Adding a full CRM
- Adding SMS campaigns
- Adding paid ads setup
- Rewriting the entire site after approval
- Unlimited back-and-forth edits

These items can be quoted separately.

## How To Submit Revisions

Please submit revisions in one organized message or document.

Recommended format:

1. Page or section name
2. Current text or issue
3. Requested change
4. Any replacement copy or image

Example:

- Page: Homepage
- Section: Hero
- Current text: "Book a Free Consultation"
- Requested change: "Start With a Free Consultation"
